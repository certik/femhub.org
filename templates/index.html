The Hermes project

Hermes is a free C++/Python library for rapid prototyping of adaptive FEM and hp-FEM solvers available under the GPL license (Version 2, 1991). The library has a clean design and modular structure. There are several unique features that make Hermes different from other FEM and hp-FEM codes.

Our mission: To make adaptive hp-FEM available to the broad computational science and engineering community as well as to undergraduate and graduate students. We want Hermes to become an open source alternatice to commercial FEM codes.


Hermes over the web: If you don't want to install hermes, you can compute remotely on our computers!
