The FEMhub project

Installation

Download, unpack, build: 

$ wget http://sage.math.washington.edu/home/ondrej/scratch/femhub/femhub-0.9.3.tar
$ tar xf femhub-0.9.3.tar
$ cd femhub-0.9.3
$ make

Depending on the speed of your computer, wait between 37 minutes to 1.5 hour. That's it.
