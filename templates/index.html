The Hermes project

Hermes is a free C++/Python library for rapid prototyping of adaptive FEM and hp-FEM solvers developed by an open source community around the hp-FEM group at the University of Nevada, Reno. The library has a clean design and modular structure, and it is available under the GPL license (Version 2, 1991). There are several unique features that make Hermes different from other FEM and hp-FEM codes.
