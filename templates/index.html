hp-FEM

hp-FEM is a modern version of the Finite Element Method (FEM), which is superior to standard (low-order) FEM due to its extremely fast exponential convergence. The hp-FEM is more demanding than standard FEM from both the mathematical and programming point of view. Therefore, hp-FEM codes are rather scarce.

Our mission is to disseminate the hp-FEM broadly and to create an open-source environment that eventually will become an alternative to old commercial FEM codes.
