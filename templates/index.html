The Hermes project

Hermes is a free C++/Python library for rapid prototyping of adaptive FEM and hp-FEM solvers available under the GPL license (Version 2, 1991). The library has a clean design and modular structure. There are several unique features that make Hermes different from other FEM and hp-FEM codes.

Home pages of the 1D, 2D and 3D versions of Hermes:

  • Hermes1D
  • Hermes2D
  • Hermes3D
Hermes over the web: Do not install Hermes, compute remotely on our computers!
