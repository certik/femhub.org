Our mission: To create an open-source distribution featuring many finite element codes, along with a web notebook and a unified Python interface. We want FEMhub to become an alternative to commercial FEM codes.

Mailing list: Please use this mailing list for all communications related to FEMhub.

How to join: See a list of nice project ideas and a list of simple-to-fix issues related to FEMhub.

Research articles about Hermes

Hermes is based on original research results. The following list of publications related to Hermes may be slightly outdated as the research goes on. For most papers, manuscripts can be downloaded from Pavel Solin's home page.

Books
  • P. Solin: Partial Differential Equations and the Finite Element Method, J. Wiley & Sons, 2005.
  • P. Solin, K. Segeth, I. Dolezel: Higher-Order Finite Element Methods, Chapman & Hall / CRC Press, 2003.
Refereed journal papers
